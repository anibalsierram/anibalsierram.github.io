RESEARCH

Research: Protoplanetary Disks

I study protoplanetary disks using ALMA and VLA observations. I am specially interested in disks with vortices, spiral arms, hints of planets, and/or rings structures. My previous work has mainly focused on study the effects of scattering on the dust continuum emission, visibility modeling, radiative transfer modeling, gas kinematics and emission heights.
